  • Endolith; Interloper is a third person hardcore tactical survival game set in a fictional relentless world inside the apocalyptic Walled City of Kowloon. You play as Mei. A low-level Interloper employed to carry out deliveries from one province to another. Death is permanent, manage your resource well, keep your limbs intact and shear those in your way or die trying. You serve the Lilies of the Valley now.
  • Its influences range from Silent Hill, S.T.A.L.K.E.R, John Wick, Doom (1993), Half-Life & Paper Mario.
